# GEMMIS LIMITED - Institutional Financial & Strategy Consultancy

![GEMMIS LIMITED Hero](gemmis_hero_bg.png)

## 🏛️ About GEMMIS LIMITED
GEMMIS LIMITED is a premier consultancy firm providing elite financial advisory, corporate finance, and strategic business solutions. We serve a diverse clientele ranging from global corporations to high-growth SMEs and startups, particularly focusing on the Nigerian and broader African markets.

## 💼 Core Service Divisions
The platform showcases 12 specialized advisory pillars:
1.  **Financial Advisory & Strategy**: Planning, modeling, and restructuring.
2.  **Corporate Finance**: M&A, capital raising, and IPO readiness.
3.  **Accounting & Reporting**: IFRS implementation and audit readiness.
4.  **Tax Advisory**: Strategic tax planning and international structuring.
5.  **Risk & Governance**: ERM, internal audit, and fraud prevention.
6.  **Treasury Management**: Cash flow and FX risk optimization.
7.  **Performance Analytics**: KPI dashboards and profitability analysis.
8.  **Investment & Wealth**: Portfolio management and estate planning.
9.  **Digital Transformation**: ERP implementation and AI process automation.
10. **Project Finance**: Infrastructure modeling and PPP advisory.
11. **SME & Startup Advisory**: Fundraising and Nigeria market entry.
12. **Specialized Niches**: Oil & Gas, Fintech, and ESG reporting.

## 🚀 Technology Stack
- **Structure**: Semantic HTML5
- **Styling**: Vanilla CSS3 (Custom Design System)
- **Logic**: ES6+ JavaScript
- **Aesthetics**: Glassmorphism, Responsive Grid, Smooth Animations
- **Assets**: Custom AI-generated high-fidelity imagery

## 🛠️ Getting Started
To run this project locally:
1. Clone the repository: `git clone https://github.com/GAJETOso/GEMMIS_Website.git`
2. Navigate to the directory: `cd GEMMIS_Website`
3. Open `index.html` in any modern web browser.
